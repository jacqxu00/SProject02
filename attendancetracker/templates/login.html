Attendance Tracker


This attendance tracker is designed for classes with lots of students (such as chorus and band). These classes usually have designated section leaders to help with attendance, and students' grades are attendance-based. This site is created to help teachers track attendance and to help students track their grades.

Created by Jackie Xu, Kevin Li, Cynthia Cheng, Giorgio Vidali. Class of 2018.
